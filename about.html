About Us

Why Choose Us

Nourishing Crops with the Right Blend of Agro-inputs

Sri Bhuvija Crop Science Pvt. Ltd. was incorporated in Hyderabad in 2012. We specialize in manufacturing high-quality micronutrient fertilizers, secondary nutrients, and other nutritional products essential for plant growth and high yields.

High Level of Publicity to Quality

We focus on overcoming nutrient deficiencies in the soil by providing the right micronutrients in the form of soil applications and foliar spray.

Serving Across Southern India

With strong roots in Telangana and Andhra Pradesh, we are expanding our branches to Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, and Maharashtra.
